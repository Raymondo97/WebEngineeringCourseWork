Our football team is nationally ranked. All team members are taught the principals of integrity, responsibility, and accountability. Students are held to a high standard of academic excellence in order to participate.

Like our football team, our soccer team members are also held to a high standard in their schooling. University University's soccer team is newly founded, starting with the Fall 2021 semester. Students involved are making history and setting the standard for the future of the team.

A high standard of class work is also a part of our volleyball team. We have both mens and womens volleyball teams. Both teams have competed on a national level, and intend to keep with that goal.
