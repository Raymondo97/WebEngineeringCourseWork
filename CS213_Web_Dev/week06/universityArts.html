Arts

The arts program here at University University is designed to include all kinds of talents, individuals, and groups. We have performing arts such as dance, theater, choir, band, and orchestra. We also have artisitic programs for painting, ceramics, and many more artistic practices.

To learn more about each different art practice, please contact our main office, who will refer you to the appropriate department heads.
